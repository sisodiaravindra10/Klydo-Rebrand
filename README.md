The complete UX/UI overhaul of Klydo, the quick-commerce fashion app for Gen Z. Mobile app, web app, brand identity, and the klydo.in marketing site — one design system, one codebase.

- Next.js 15 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind v4 with brand tokens
- Framer Motion + GSAP
- Vercel deploy

## North star

> Klydo is the fashion app for the impatient with taste.

Speed of Blinkit. Taste of *Vogue India*. Trust of an honest shopkeeper. Voice of a friend who works in fashion.

No banner stacks. No fake MRP. No coupon theatre.
