## Why this email works

- **Subject line is honest** about being unsolicited. CEOs respect candor; they ignore false urgency.
- **First sentence places you** (user, city). Second sentence flatters the product, not the person.
- **The work is the proof.** No "let me explain my vision" — just a live URL and a deck.
- **No ask beyond "chat if you want."** Removes pressure. Generosity wins more meetings than asks.
- **Closes with the larger mission**, not yourself. Leaves the reader thinking about the product.

## What to attach

- `klydo-rebrand.pdf` — the pitch deck (in this folder)
- Optionally: a short Loom video walking through the live demo

## What NOT to send

- Long resume
- Hourly rate / pricing
- "I have a few suggestions" energy
- More than 1 follow-up. If no reply in 10 days, move on.
